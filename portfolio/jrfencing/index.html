We Provide Custom Fencing Installations & Repairs Based in Staten Island, New York

At JR Fencing, we want to provide for all your fencing needs. We'll complete any project you request of us, no matter the size. When your fence or gate has been damaged, whether due to inclement weather or vandalism, we can provide you with high-quality fence repair services to ensure that your fence is back in prime condition. Our employees have many years of experience, so you can be sure that the repair and refinishing work we provide will be up to your standard. These repair services also apply to any guardrails you may have at your home or business.

With our fence installation services, your fence or other enclosure will be installed properly the first time. Although the installation of a fence can take some time, we work quickly and efficiently. We can help with any type of fence you purchase, from an electric fence to basketball court enclosures and everything in between. We also offer custom fencing services if you wish to design your own fence.

If you've purchased a fence but are looking to polish it to a better sheen, we offer a wide selection of finishing services. These services also apply to old fences or gates that you would like to change in some manner. Some of our primary finishes include the application of wax, drying oil, varnish or paint, among other options. Call us today to find out more about what we can do for you.
